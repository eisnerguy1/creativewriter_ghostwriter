The January 1995 Issue of Electronic Entertainment had a small blurb about this add-on to Creative Writer



Young fans of the PBS 
“Ghostwriter” series will be fas- 
cinated by The Case of the 
Blue Makva. A collaboration 
between the Children’s Tele- 
vision Workshop and Microsoft, 
this add-on to Creative Writer is a 
mystery series featuring the 
“Ghostwriter” characters. 

Using the floppy-based or CD 
version of Creative Writer, kids open 
up the ten mysteries and start 
sleuthing. They create a casebook 
to keep track of clues. Goodies like 
secret codes and invisible ink help 
them uncover the real identity of the 
Blue Makva. The product also pro- 
vides suggestions for related activi-
ties that kids can pursue in Creative
Writer. (Microsoft; 800-426-9400;
Windows/Mac, $19.95
